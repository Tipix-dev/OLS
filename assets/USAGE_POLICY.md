# Usage Policy

## 1. General Provisions

OLS is a toolkit of CLI utilities designed to make terminal workflows more predictable, logged, and pipeline-friendly.

The Project is currently an early-stage MVP. By using OLS, the user acknowledges and accepts that functionality may be unstable and subject to change.

---

## 2. Core Behavior

By using OLS, the user acknowledges and agrees that:

- **Actions may be logged**  
  OLS may record executed actions for traceability and debugging purposes.

- **Pipeline compatibility**  
  Tools are designed to work with standard Unix streams (`stdin`, `stdout`, `stderr`), and behavior may depend on input/output flow.

- **Internet-based package retrieval**  
  Components may be downloaded from the Internet during first use and cached for offline operation.
